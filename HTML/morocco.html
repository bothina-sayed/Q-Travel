our Tourism Attraction in marocco

Marrakesh Medina

The old city is entered from the vast plaza of Djemma el-fna Square where, it seems, half the city converges throughout the day and into the evening to hang out with the stall vendors, traditional musicians, snake charmers, and random acrobats.

Choose

Oudaias Kasbah

This is a peaceful and perfectly quaint district that feels miles away from the city.Inside the walls of this old fortress, the lanes of neat white-and-blue houses rimmed by colorful flowerpots and flapping washing have a lost-in-time atmosphere that's hard to beat.

Choose

Hassan II Mosque

the Hassan II Mosque is a lavish symbol not only of the city, but also of Morocco itself. This modern mosque (finished in 1993) doesn't do things by halves.

Choose

Tangier

The most European of all Morocco's cities, Tangier has a fascinating and slightly debauched role in 20th-century literary history, and this past is what draws many tourists here.
